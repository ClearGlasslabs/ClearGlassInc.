# Strategic Alignment

ClearGlass aligns strongly with the NIST AI RMF because the platform architecture assumes:

- AI systems require operational governance
- Autonomous behavior requires bounded execution
- Runtime observability is mandatory
- Compliance enforcement must occur inline
- Human escalation remains necessary for high-risk operations

The platform therefore positions itself as:

> Governed Operational AI Infrastructure aligned to enterprise AI risk management principles.
